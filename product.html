What we will cover in Wine 101

  • This class will focus on white, rose and red wines, wine tastings included
  • Basics of grape growing
  • Basics of red wine making
  • Comparing Old World and new World Wines
  • Understand wine styles (acid, sweetness, tannin, body)
  • Breaking the myths of serving order
  • Learn proper serving temperature depending on wine type
  • Basic stemware shapes
  • The principles of proper wine and food pairing
  • Wine Tasting - how to see, smell, taste
  • Learn basic wine speak

What we will cover in Wine 102

  • A great match with Intro to Wine 101! Wine 101 only covers HALF of the different styles of wine, this class covers the other half
  • This class will let you taste Sparkling (including Champagne), Fortified and Late Harvest wines, wine tastings included
  • How sparkling wine (including Champagne) is made
  • Key grapes in Sparkling, Fortified and Late Harvest wines
  • Understanding the styles of sparkling wine
  • Learn the types of late harvest wines
  • What is Noble Rot
  • Understand the styles of fortified wines, including Port
